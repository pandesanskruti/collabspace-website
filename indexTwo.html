FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions!

What is Collabspace?

Collabspace is a collaborative platform designed to enhance teamwork and communication through features like audio and video calls, screen sharing, and gaming integration.

01

How do I create an account on Collabspace?

To create an account, visit the Collabspace homepage and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the prompts to enter your details and verify your email.

02

Is there a limit on the number of participants in a call?

The free plan has a participant limit, while premium plans allow for larger groups. Check the pricing page for specific limits on each plan.

03

How do I invite others to join my session?

You can invite others by sending them a unique session link generated within the app. Simply share the link via email or messaging apps.

04

Can I use Collabspace for gaming?

Yes! Collabspace supports game integration, allowing you to play games with friends while using the platform for communication.

05

What features are available in the free plan?

The free plan includes basic features such as audio calls, video calls, and screen sharing, while premium plans offer additional features like advanced file sharing and larger group limits.
